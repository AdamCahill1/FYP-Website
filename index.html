An Anti-Mileage Fraud System for Odometer Integrity and Vehicle Value Protection

Welcome to my Final Year Project landing page. the goal of this project is to explore and implement secure communication methods within vehicle networks specifically focusing on preventing mileage tampering. I will do this by using Secure Onboard Communication (SecOC) and encryption techniques. I aim to enhance data integrity and security in modern vehicles. This will help ensure that mileage readings remain accurate, preventing fraud and maintaining trust in vehicle diagnostics and resale markets. Additionally the project will examine how cloud-based solutions can further protect against manipulation, providing a comprehensive approach to securing vehicle data with modern technology and features in vehicles.
